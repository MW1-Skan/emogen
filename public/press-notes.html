Emogen — AI Emoji Finder for Flexible Searches

Emogen (https://emo-gen.org) is an AI-powered emoji lookup for people frustrated by rigid native pickers. Enter a word, feeling, or full expression and Emogen replies with three creative emojis you can copy, favorite, or revisit from history.

  • No accounts, no paywalls — optional donations keep hosting and the Genkit/OpenAI flow running.

Specs

  • Framework: Angular 20 + Tailwind + PrimeNG overlays.
  • AI: Genkit flow hitting OpenAI gpt-4o-mini.
  • Hosting: Vercel static frontend + Firebase Functions for the Genkit flow.
  • Persistence: localStorage history (10 slots) + favorites with dedupe.
  • Accessibility: keyboard-friendly controls, focus states, reduced-motion support.
  • SEO: canonical metadata, Open Graph cards, sitemap, FAQ block, SoftwareApplication schema.
