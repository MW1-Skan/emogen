Emogen – AI Emoji Generator

Emogen (https://emo-gen.org) turns any written vibe into a three-emoji mood board powered by Genkit + OpenAI. The Angular 20 frontend is desktop-first, animated, and ships with localStorage history, favorites, and one-tap copy.

Key Talking Points

  • AI emoji generator tuned for Discord/Slack/Social replies.
  • Floating emoji background, tactile buttons, regen animation, toast feedback for delightful UX.
  • Copy, favorite, and revisit emoji sets stored in localStorage.
  • No accounts, no paywalls — optional donations support hosting.

Specs

  • Framework: Angular 20 + Tailwind + PrimeNG overlays.
  • AI: Genkit flow hitting OpenAI gpt-4o-mini.
  • Hosting: Vercel static frontend on https://emo-gen.org.
  • Persistence: localStorage history (10 slots) + favorites with dedupe.
  • Accessibility: zoneless change detection, semantics, keyboard-friendly controls.
  • SEO: canonical metadata, Open Graph cards, sitemap, FAQ block, SoftwareApplication schema.

Suggested Angles

  1. “AI emoji generator for community managers and streamers.”
  2. “Lightweight inspiration board for feelings, product launches, or vibe checks.”
  3. “Clipboard-friendly emoji workflow for Discord mods or social teams.”
